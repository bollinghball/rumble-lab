How to Apply a Badge:

Please note: The instructions below are to be done by an adult and/or with adult supervision

  • 1. Carefully cut inside dotted circle. The white will show up so the neater you cut, the better your badge will look
  • 2. Preheat iron to the highest setting. DO NOT use any steam.
  • 3. Place ironing board between two sides of shirt. This keeps you from melting the ink on the other side of the shirt.
  • 4. Place your badge ink side up in the desired location.
  • 5. Cut and place parchment paper over the badge.
  • 6. DO NOT let the iron touch the graphic on the shirt.
  • 7. Apply iron to badge for roughly 40 seconds.
  • 8. Celebrate and unlock more badges!
