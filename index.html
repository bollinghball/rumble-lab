Who is Rumble?

Rumble is a suit. The suit is an icon that belongs to the most impressive young engineering mind that we are aware of. Right now, Rumble belongs to Team Askins. Team Askins is currently on our Mission to Mars and is absolutely rocking it.

RumbleLab was founded by Moultrie Ball. Moultrie is a mechanical engineer by trade but more comfortably identifis as a big kid. Growing up, school wasn't his thing but engineering was. We started RumbleLab for two reasons. First, to train kids to unleash their inner engineer. And second, to highlight the rockstars we encounter. Think your kid has what it takes? We do!
